About us

BR Metal Works?

BR Metal Works is a company that specializes in metal steel and aluminum works. As a team of skilled professionals with years of experience in the industry, we pride ourselves on delivering high-quality products and services using state-of-the-art equipment and technology. At BR Metal Works, we prioritize customer satisfaction by providing personalized service, listening to your needs, and ensuring timely delivery of your products. We believe that building strong relationships with our customers is key to our success.

What we provide?

Iron Strucutre

We are able to build all kinds of iron structures and perform a wide range of iron construction projects. Our services include the design and construction of metal staircases, balconies, railings, gates, and fences. We are also equipped to build and install metal structures such as warehouses, workshops, and hangars.

Steel Construction

With our comprehensive services, we are capable of building all kinds of steel structures and performing a wide range of steel construction projects. Our services include the design, fabrication, and installation of steel structures such as warehouses, workshops, hangars, and steel buildings for commercial and residential purposes. We also specialize in constructing steel bridges, steel tanks, and other heavy steel structures.

Aluminium Construction

With our years of experience and skilled workforce, we can build all kinds of aluminum structures, from commercial to residential buildings. Our services include the construction of aluminum curtain walls, windows, doors, facades, and storefronts. Our also specialize in the installation of aluminum cladding, roofing, and siding, providing long-lasting and durable protection for buildings.

Polycarbonate and Upvc Strucutre

With our years of experience, we can build all kinds of polycarbonate and Upvc Structures, from commercial to residential buildings. Our services include the construction of polycarbonate roofs, Upvc windows, doors, facades, and storefronts. Our also specialize in the installation of Upvc cladding, roofing, and siding, providing long-lasting and durable protection for buildings.

Glass Block Strucutre

With our years of experience and skilled workforce, we can build all kinds of Glass structures too, from commercial to residential buildings from low lwvwl to high level. Our services include the construction of glass curtain walls, windows, doors, facades, and storefronts. Our also specialize in the installation of glass railing, roofing, and siding, providing long-lasting and durable protection for buildings.
