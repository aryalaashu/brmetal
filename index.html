About us

BR Metal Works?

As we being subsidiary company of Sigmacon, we specializes in metal, steel, glass, and aluminum works. As part of the Sigmacon family of businesses, we benefit from the resources and expertise of a larger organization, while still maintaining our focus on delivering high-quality products and services to our customers.

At BR Metal Works, we pride ourselves on our skilled professionals, who bring years of experience in the industry and a commitment to excellence to every project. We use state-of-the-art equipment and technology to ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality and durability.

But we know that technology alone isn't enough to deliver great results. That's why we prioritize customer satisfaction, and strive to provide personalized service to every client. We listen to your needs and work closely with you to ensure that your project is completed on time and to your exact specifications. We believe that building strong relationships with our customers is key to our success, and we're committed to earning your trust and your business.

What we provide?

Iron Strucutre

we are capable of building all kinds of iron structures and performing a wide range of iron construction projects. Our services include the design, fabrication, and installation of iron structures such as warehouses, workshops, hangars, and steel buildings for commercial and residential purposes. We also specialize in constructing iron bridges, iron heavy poles, and other heavy iron structures.

Steel Construction

With our comprehensive services, we are capable of building all kinds of steel structures and performing a wide range of steel construction projects. Our services include the design, fabrication, and installation of steel structures such as warehouses, workshops, hangars, and steel buildings for commercial and residential purposes. We also specialize in constructing steel bridges, steel tanks, and other heavy steel structures.

Aluminium Construction

we can build all kinds of aluminum structures, from commercial to residential buildings. Our services include the construction of aluminum curtain walls, windows, doors, facades, and storefronts. Our also specialize in the installation of aluminum cladding, roofing, and siding, providing long-lasting and durable protection for buildings.

Polycarbonate and Upvc Strucutre

we are experts to build all kinds of polycarbonate and Upvc Structures, from commercial to residential buildings. Our services include the construction of polycarbonate roofs, Upvc windows, doors, facades, and storefronts. Our also specialize in the installation of Upvc cladding, roofing, and siding, providing long-lasting and durable protection for buildings.

Glass Block Strucutre

With our years of experience and skilled workforce, we can build all kinds of Glass structures too, from commercial to residential buildings from low levell to high level. Our services include the construction of glass curtain walls, windows, doors, facades, and storefronts. Our also specialize in the installation of glass railing, roofing, and siding, providing long-lasting and durable protection for buildings.
